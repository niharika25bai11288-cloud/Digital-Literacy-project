Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

 General Safety Tips:-
* Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts
* Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible
* Avoid clicking on unknown links or suspicious emails
* Do not share personal information on public platforms
* Keep your devices and apps updated regularly
* Be cautious while using public Wi-Fi networks

 Financial / UPI Safety Tips:-
* Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or bank details with anyone
* Do not scan unknown QR codes or approve unknown payment requests
* Always verify the receiver’s details before sending money

 Reporting Cybercrime:-
* Report cybercrime immediately on the National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
* Call the Cyber Crime Helpline Number: 1930 for financial fraud assistance

 Conclusion:-
Following these precautions can help college students stay safe online and protect themselves from cyber 
threats.

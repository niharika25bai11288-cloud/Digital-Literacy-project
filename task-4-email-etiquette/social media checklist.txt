Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist


 Do’s (Things You Should Do)
 *Think before you post anything online
 *Maintain a professional and respectful tone
 *Protect your personal information (phone number, address, etc.)
 *Use privacy settings to control who can see your content
 *Share useful, positive, and informative content


 Don’ts (Things You Should Avoid)
* Do not share fake news or unverified information
*Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content
 *Do not engage in online arguments or cyberbullying
 *Avoid oversharing personal or sensitive information Do not post anything that can harm your reputation


 Conclusion
Responsible use of social media helps maintain a positive digital identity and ensures safe and respectful online
 interactions.
